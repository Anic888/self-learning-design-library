## Why I care
**Queen of type-as-image editorial**. Paula shows how typography alone — cropped, layered, colored with conviction — can carry an entire identity. Reference when project needs bold hand-lettered / custom-type energy without illustration or photography. Fills the "American commercial typographic maximalism" genre gap (between Swiss restraint and Carson chaos).
