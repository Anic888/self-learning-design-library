## Why I care
Legendary **Swiss modernism applied at American commercial scale**. "The Life of a Designer is a Life of Fight" — his book. Believed design language should be small (6 typefaces) and deep (mastered systemically). Reference for **ruthless discipline**, wayfinding logic, systems thinking, and the philosophy that constraints produce better work.

Legacy reference like Syd Mead — deceased 2014, so no "live refinement" possible, but his work is foundational to modern graphic design.
