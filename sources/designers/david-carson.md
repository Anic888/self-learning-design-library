## Why I care
Легендарный **grid-breaking typographer**. "Don't mistake legibility for communication" — его дизайн-манифест. Reference когда нужен ANTI-clean anti-corporate язык: chaos typography, intentional illegibility, gestural/emotional layouts. Полная противоположность Swiss Design / Sagmeister concept-focus.
