## Why I care
Reference for **Japanese modern minimalism through Western reach**. Not zen-cliché — Hara's work is philosophically grounded in emptiness (ma), materiality, and sensory design. Fills genre gap between Swiss restraint (Vignelli) and Western minimal (Massimo Vignelli) on one side, and between hard modernism and traditional Japanese aesthetic on the other.

Unique value: his approach to **white space as active material**, not residual.
