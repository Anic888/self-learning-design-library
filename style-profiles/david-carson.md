# David Carson — Style Profile

## Core Identity
**Anti-grid rebellion.** "Don't mistake legibility for communication." Typography as emotion, imperfection as intent, chaos as a compositional strategy. Ray Gun magazine aesthetic extended to everything.

## Palette
- **Highly variable per project**, но tendencies:
  - High-contrast monochrome (black + white + one messy accent)
  - Pre-press / print artifacts (CMYK misregistration, halftone bleeds)
  - Washed-out photo colors (underexposed, saturation lost)
- Often **3-4 colors но roughly applied** — splatters, overlaps, bleeds
- **Black и white доминируют** в 60-70% работ

## Typography
- **TOTALLY chaos-based** — it's the point
- Font mixing: 4-6 different fonts на одной page (heresy elsewhere, here it's doctrine)
- Script + serif + grunge + display в одной работе
- **Letters отдельно от слов** — tracking может быть 200px (letters floating separately)
- **Overlapping type** — слова lie on top of each other
- **Type as texture** — small illegible blocks of text forming shape
- **Wrong-way type** — sideways, upside down, rotating
- **Scribble on type** — drawing on/over text manually

## Composition & Grid
- **NO grid.** Or broken grid. Or grid that exists only to be violated.
- **Random element placement** — но не truly random, intentional disruption
- **Overlapping layers** — everything on top of everything
- **Tilted elements** — nothing straight
- **Texture over structure** — forget rule of thirds
- **White space used chaotically** — not as rest, as drama

## Signature Moves
- **Xerox distressed imagery** (photocopied to death)
- **Text running off page** intentionally
- **Title hidden** в corner, tiny, below fold
- **Large photo misaligned** — bleeds, crops, wrong framing
- **Hand-drawn arrows / scribbles** over designed pieces
- **Wrong fonts** purposefully (Zapf Dingbats for Bryan Ferry interview)
- **Distorted typography** — stretched, squeezed, sliced
- **Fragmented language** — words broken across page
- **CMYK misregistration** — 4-color printing gone wrong (or "right")

## Techniques / Tools
- **90s print workflow** — originally Quark XPress + photocopier + manual paste-up
- Photography (often his own)
- Hand-drawn elements scanned in
- Digital now, но aesthetic remains pre-digital
- **Physical collage sensibility** even when digital

## Mood / Emotional Register
Rebellious, intuitive, emotional, anti-corporate. Youth culture, alternative music, rejecting clean. Evokes: rock'n'roll, punk, underground zines.

## When to apply
- ✅ Alternative magazines, zines
- ✅ Music packaging (indie rock, punk, experimental)
- ✅ Youth culture brands
- ✅ Когда проект требует FEELING over FUNCTION
- ✅ Когда все другие approaches feel generic
- ❌ НЕ для corporate (в принципе)
- ❌ НЕ для wayfinding / UX (people die на illegible signage)
- ❌ НЕ для information-heavy work (cenze gets lost)

## How to apply (tactical для Claude)
1. **Break the grid.** Place hero text off-center, tilted, partially off-page.
2. **Mix 4+ fonts** на одной work. Contrast heavy slab + script + grunge display.
3. **Overlap everything** — text on images, text on text, images on text.
4. **Leave "mistakes"** in — misalignment, cutoffs, blur.
5. **Add hand-drawn marks** — arrows, underlines, scribbles, x's.
6. **Texture heavy** — halftone, xerox, grunge, scanline (как artifact, не decoration).
7. **Hide main message** — put headline tiny в corner, large illegible type в middle.
8. **Tilt things** — 5-15° rotations на random elements.
9. **Photography should look "wrong"** — overexposed, cropped weirdly, faded.
10. **НЕ использовать:** clean sans-serif headline в center, balanced composition, modular grid, flat even color. All these signify what Carson rejects.
