# Beeple (Mike Winkelmann) — Style Profile

## Core Identity
Daily-practice 3D digital satire. High volume + political/cultural commentary через absurd juxtapositions. Polarizing — loved и hated в equal measure.

## Palette
- **Highly variable** — every Everyday is different
- **Tendencies:** high saturation, neon highlights, industrial greys as base
- Frequent pairings: `#FF0099` + `#00FFFF` (neon), `#FFD500` + `#0066FF`, или gory red + industrial grey
- **Обычно 2-3 saturated colors против desaturated background**

## Typography
- **Minimal / absent** в большинстве работ
- Когда есть: **all caps stencil / military** или absurdist sign text
- Typography как часть mockery — political signs, ironic brand names
- НЕ typography-forward работы

## Composition & Grid
- **Central subject dominance** — обычно один центральный объект (building, figure, vehicle)
- **Wide vista compositions** — часто 16:9 cinematic
- **3D rendered perspective** — всё с глубиной
- **Low horizon** часто — делает subjects огромными

## Signature Moves
- **3D renders с "dirty" / weathered текстурой**
- **Absurd scale** — огромные объекты / fine детали в неожиданных местах
- **Political/cultural figures** из pop culture mashup
- **Industrial/machinery aesthetic** — trucks, factories, grime
- **Neon signs как культурный marker** (McDonald's, USA flags, corporate logos modified)
- **Nude / body horror** (often with NSFW content)
- **Text signs mocking politics / tech / celebrities**

## Techniques / Tools
- **Cinema 4D** (primary — 90%+ работ)
- Octane / Redshift для rendering
- **Speed-focused** — daily constraint shapes his process

## Mood / Emotional Register
Satirical, angry, exhausted, earnest-ironic. Mixed: humor + horror + political commentary. Не меланхолический как Thorp или optimistic как Signalnoise. Ближе к editorial cartoon в 3D.

## When to apply
- ✅ Satirical commentary, political art
- ✅ Bold 3D-forward composition
- ✅ Pop culture mashups
- ✅ "Daily art" practice examples
- ❌ НЕ для subtle / refined dizajn
- ❌ НЕ для brand / corporate / client work обычно
- ❌ НЕ если хочется "timeless" — работы очень топикально привязаны

## How to apply (tactical для Claude)
1. **Композиция: one massive 3D subject** в центре, detailed environment around.
2. **Палитра: saturated neon accents** против industrial grey/brown base.
3. **Weathered textures** — grime, rust, wear на всём.
4. **Absurd juxtaposition** — что-то невероятное в realistic setting.
5. **Cinema 4D look** — DOF, GI lighting, motion blur hints.
6. **Signs / text как pop culture commentary** — modified brand logos, ironic slogans.
7. **Scale play** — либо tiny humans near huge objects, либо reverse.
8. **НЕ использовать:** clean graphic design language, subtle palettes, minimalist compositions. Beeple — not about restraint.

## Notes from live verification (2026-04-15)

Visited beeple-crap.com — observations:

- **Site itself** is atmospheric/contemplative (forest with single vertical light beam), contrasting with loud individual pieces — his self-presentation is moodier than his content

- **Self-deprecating voice:** bio reads "he makes a variety of art crap across a variety of media. some of it is ok, but a lot of it kind of blows ass. he's working on making it suck less everyday though so bear with him..." — this tone matters. Not pretentious even at Christie's-$69M level.

Implications for the profile:

- **The atmospheric/dark side of his palette** is under-represented in the seed profile — he does quiet/atmospheric work too, not only loud satire
