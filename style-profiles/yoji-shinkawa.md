# Yoji Shinkawa — Style Profile

## Core Identity
**Sumi-e traditional ink painting applied to sci-fi/mecha**. Wet brushwork, gestural confidence, monochrome discipline. Ink on rice paper × Metal Gear Solid.

## Palette
- `#0E0E0E` / `#181818` — ink black (dominant, 50-70%)
- `#F2EDE0` / `#FBF8F0` — rice paper off-white (base, 30-40%)
- `#8B2418` / `#A33020` — deep oxblood red (rare signature accent, 2-5%)
- Occasionally: **sepia wash** (`#5C4028`) для aged traditional feel
- **Тенденция:** almost strictly monochromatic. Red используется ONLY как symbolic accent (eye glow, insignia, blood). Никаких neon, никаких cool cyber-палитр.

## Typography
- **Почти отсутствует в его art-works.** Когда есть:
  - Japanese calligraphy (kanji brushed as part of composition)
  - Technical stenciled labels (military-style) — sparse, functional
  - Latin text: сжатый sans / typewriter style
- **Typography as artifact, not design element**. Он illustrator, not designer.

## Composition & Grid
- **Single subject dominance** — mecha / character в центре композиции
- **Dynamic gestural positioning** — never static poses
- **Negative space as power** — large areas of white/untouched paper
- **Diagonal energy** — движение/тенсия through composition
- **Zen-inspired asymmetry** — ma (間) — purposeful emptiness

## Signature Moves
- **Wet ink brushstrokes** — visible brush hairs, water bleed, ink pooling
- **Splatter/drip** — controlled "accidents" as energy indication
- **Organic rendering of mechanical** — mecha feel ALIVE не robotic
- **High-contrast silhouettes** — subject as ink shape rather than detailed rendering
- **Brush calligraphy** techniques (flick, wet-dry transitions)
- **Traditional mount format** — иногда long vertical scroll proportions
- **Red seal/stamp** — сamaculture signature element
- **Smoke/mist** through ink wash — атмосфера through dilution

## Techniques / Tools
- **Traditional sumi ink** on rice paper (primary medium — REAL paint, не digital!)
- Natural hair brushes (various sizes)
- Some digital finishing (Photoshop) для game production
- Occasionally: watercolor washes, sepia ink
- Process: bold gesture FIRST, details added through overlapping strokes

## Mood / Emotional Register
Meditative, powerful, restrained. East Asian ink tradition emotion: quiet dignity, power in simplicity, energy through gesture. Not anime-cute, не western-muscular — а **contemplative martial**.

## When to apply
- ✅ Mecha / military sci-fi
- ✅ Character studies
- ✅ Book covers нуждающиеся в gestural power
- ✅ Poster art где нужен East Asian traditional feel
- ✅ Когда бизнес-логика говорит "minimal colour palette, maximum impact"
- ❌ НЕ для glossy commercial
- ❌ НЕ для corporate identity
- ❌ НЕ для multi-color illustrations

## How to apply (tactical for Claude)
1. **Палитра: ink black + off-white paper ONLY.** Optional red accent (max 5%).
2. **Композиция: ONE hero figure** (mecha / character / abstract form) в движении.
3. **Massive negative space** — 40-60% untouched "paper".
4. **Brushstroke simulation** — use varying stroke widths (3px thin to 40px brush), jagged edges, не clean smooth paths.
5. **Wet edges** — некоторые strokes blur/bleed на краях (через low-opacity layered paths или blur filter).
6. **Splatter dots** — add 15-30 small irregular dots вокруг main figure (ink splash feel).
7. **Red seal** — добавь small red square/stamp в нижнем углу как signature.
8. **НЕТ градиентов** (except ink wash implying fade/atmosphere).
9. **НЕ использовать:** neon colors, clean vector shapes, corporate palette, photographic textures. Only: ink + paper.
