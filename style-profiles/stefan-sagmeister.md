# Stefan Sagmeister — Style Profile

## Core Identity
**Concept-first design.** Каждая работа starts with an idea, not with a style. "What's the most interesting way to communicate this?" — and the answer is rarely stock template. Material exploration + conceptual wit + obsessive craft.

## Palette
- **Per-project determined** — палитра выбирается как argument, не decoration
- **Tendencies:**
  - Often monochromatic + one accent (black/white + red)
  - Hand-painted / hand-rendered colors (not digitally pure)
  - Yellow as signature safe-choice (Happy Show bright yellow)
  - Muted printing ink tones
- **Rule:** palette should serve concept, not aesthetic

## Typography
- **Hand-lettered often** — carved, painted, stuck, arranged with objects
- **Typography as main visual element** чаще чем imagery
- **Custom solutions per project** — rarely re-uses type system
- **Mixing hand-drawn with typeset** — sharp contrast
- **Word as image** — letter becomes object / object becomes letter
- **All caps common** для display (hand-drawn in caps reads stronger)

## Composition & Grid
- **Conceptual layout** — композиция serves idea
- Often **single-focal-point dominance** — one big message
- **Centered / symmetric** when concept calls for monumentality
- **Asymmetric** when motion/narrative required
- **Generous scale variation** — huge next to tiny

## Signature Moves
- **Unusual materials** — skin, food, paper cut-outs, found objects, body
- **Craft visible** — see the hand, see the effort
- **Humor + intelligence** — serious ideas делаются playful execution
- **Photograph of thing made** — often the poster IS a photo of carefully arranged objects
- **Typography из objects** — letters made from coffee, tape, paper, bodies
- **Long-running series** — Happiness, Things I Have Learned, etc
- **Minimal digital manipulation** — keep raw, keep physical
- **Big wall-scale work** — murals, installations, environmental graphics

## Techniques / Tools
- **Physical making first** — objects, photographs of constructed scenes
- Photoshop для final assembly, но не для style
- Custom lettering (hand-drawn, cut, built)
- Collaboration with specialized craftsmen (carvers, sculptors, embroiderers)

## Mood / Emotional Register
Witty, generous, humanist, intellectually playful. Big ideas made accessible. Не cold analytical (Swiss), не chaotic emotional (Carson), но warm thoughtful.

## When to apply
- ✅ Brand campaigns needing strong concept
- ✅ Cultural institutions (museums, festivals)
- ✅ Book covers / album covers где concept > genre
- ✅ Когда project budget allows for physical production
- ✅ Когда ты хочешь сделать SOMETHING UNIQUE, не variant of template
- ❌ НЕ для daily digital content (too bespoke to scale)
- ❌ НЕ для speed-first projects
- ❌ НЕ для pure decorative work (он always says something)

## How to apply (tactical для Claude)
1. **Start with "what's the idea?"** — НЕ "what should it look like?"
2. **Choose material/gesture** that embodies the idea (e.g., for "fragile trust" → poster on torn paper).
3. **Use typography as the main image** — custom hand-lettered word большой, всё остальное support.
4. **Palette: 2-3 colors max.** Restrain — concept carries weight.
5. **Photograph physical things** когда possible — or simulate photography (DOF, shadows, paper texture, material feel).
6. **Keep craft visible** — imperfections welcome (marker bleed, uneven cut, finger shadow).
7. **One clear message** — if poster has 5 things, cut 3.
8. **Surprising scale** — main element может быть huge ИЛИ tiny, never "reasonable".
9. **Question convention** — if category says "use this typeface", try the opposite.
10. **НЕ использовать:** generic template compositions, stock imagery, clean digital-born look. Sagmeister — о making.
