# Kilian Eng — Style Profile

## Core Identity
Swedish maximalist "graphics artist" (his self-description). Mondo poster tradition + dark fantasy + monochrome engraving. **Digital medium that mimics traditional printmaking** (screenprint, engraving, etching) depending on mood. Operates in both lush color and stark B&W registers.

## Palette
**Verified live via Instagram @kilianeng 2026-04:**

Kilian operates in **three distinct palette modes**, selecting per piece:

### Mode 1 — Warm Mondo (70s Heavy Metal lineage)
- `#D95C2A` / `#E07E3B` — warm orange / burnt sienna
- `#C49220` / `#D9A832` — mustard yellow
- `#6B3E8C` / `#4E2A6B` — dusty purple (night skies)
- `#F2E9D8` / `#E8DCC0` — warm cream paper
- `#3B6B4D` / `#2C5240` — forest green
- Example: "The Dark Crystal" poster — ornate fantasy in this register

### Mode 2 — Monochrome Engraving
- `#0E0E0E` / `#1A1A1A` — deep ink black
- `#F5F0E6` / `#EAE3D1` — warm bone white paper
- Scratchboard / wood-engraving aesthetic — **no color, pure line/texture**
- Example: Monochrome rocky landscape pieces (common in his recent IG)

### Mode 3 — Cold Dark Fantasy
- `#1A2540` / `#0E1728` — deep navy/midnight
- `#4A6B8A` / `#3D5A73` — misty blue-gray
- `#8B6F4F` / `#6B5440` — warm desaturated brown
- Rare red accent (`#A33525`) for figures/creatures
- Example: Underwater astronaut piece, misty canyon fantasy

**Rule:** he picks ONE mode per piece. Does not mix modes within a single work. Discipline within each mode is tight — 3-5 colors when color, zero colors when B&W.

## Typography
- **Minimal in the artwork itself** — typography usually external (movie title above/below, concert date at bottom)
- When used in piece:
  - **70s sci-fi geometric sans** (Eurostile, ITC Avant Garde, Futura) — for sci-fi subjects
  - **Ornamental art nouveau serif** — for fantasy subjects
  - **Occasionally hand-drawn "film title"** treatment (Dark Crystal poster)
- **Classic movie poster layout** — title positioned top or bottom, cast/credits at base

## Composition & Grid
- **Hero subject centered** — vehicle/creature/figure/architecture dominates
- **Ornamental frame/border** common (verified on multiple works) — geometric, art-nouveau, or architectural motifs as containers
- **Horizon two-thirds down** — massive sky territory
- **Tiny silhouetted figure for scale** — almost always present in landscape pieces (verified: astronauts, riders, walkers)
- **Dense midground** — multiple details to discover
- **Layered atmospheric depth** via color saturation (distant things desaturate)
- **Vertical poster proportions** dominant

## Signature Moves
- **Screen-print / engraving grain** — visible halftone, lithograph texture, dot patterns
- **Color separation artifacts** — simulated CMYK misregistration on Mode 1 work
- **Ornamental frames/borders** — geometric, celtic, art-nouveau motifs around focal area
- **Multiple celestial bodies** — 2 suns, planets, moons in skies (more common in Mode 1)
- **Retro-sci-fi + dark fantasy hybrid** — spaceships AND castles in same body of work
- **Silhouetted tiny figure** for scale against huge environments
- **Dithered gradients** — stepped, not smooth (screenprint limit simulation)
- **Black ink crosshatching** (Mode 2) — dense linework, woodcut/engraving references
- **Painterly rendering** even when evoking print — not vector-flat

## Mood / Emotional Register
- **Mode 1 (Mondo):** nostalgic wonder, cinematic scale, 70s sci-fi romance
- **Mode 2 (Engraving):** ancient, austere, timeless, mythological weight
- **Mode 3 (Dark fantasy):** melancholy mystery, contemplative isolation, lovecraftian adjacent

All modes share: **reverent stillness**. Subject is vast, viewer is small. Never playful, never cynical.

## When to apply
- ✅ Alternative movie posters (genre film marketing — Mondo tradition)
- ✅ Book covers for sci-fi and fantasy
- ✅ Album art for metal, folk, progressive music
- ✅ Gallery prints / art editions
- ✅ When you want printmaking aesthetic without actual printmaking
- ✅ When project needs ornamental density + narrative depth
- ❌ NOT for corporate / clean modern
- ❌ NOT for cold cyber (Thorp's zone)
- ❌ NOT for minimalist (Kilian is ornamental)
- ❌ NOT for bright synthwave (Signalnoise's zone)

## How to apply (tactical for Claude)
1. **Pick ONE of three modes:** Warm Mondo / Monochrome Engraving / Cold Dark Fantasy. Do not mix.
2. **Hero subject centered** — creature/vehicle/architecture/figure dominates frame.
3. **Ornamental frame/border** — geometric or art-nouveau container around focal area.
4. **Add tiny silhouetted figure** for scale against massive subject/environment.
5. **Horizon at 60-70% down** — huge sky territory.
6. **Multiple celestial bodies** if sci-fi mode (2 suns, moons, planets in skies).
7. **Grain/halftone overlay** on everything — imitate screenprint or engraving texture.
8. **Dithered gradients** — stepped, not smooth.
9. **Film/poster layout** — title top OR bottom, credits at base, subject in middle.
10. **Dense midground** — multiple visual points to discover, not minimalist composition.
11. **If Mode 2 (engraving):** pure black ink on warm bone-white paper, crosshatching, woodcut references, ZERO color.
12. **NOT to use:** modern flat vector without texture, clean monochrome minimalism, neon cyberpunk palette, photographic realism.
