# Kilian Eng — Style Profile

## Core Identity
70s Heavy Metal magazine meets modern Mondo poster tradition. Retrofuturism through **screen-print sensibility** — limited color separations, warm nostalgic palettes, ornamental density. Heir to Moebius без Франции.

## Palette
- `#D95C2A` / `#E07E3B` — warm orange / burnt sienna (signature accent, 20-30%)
- `#2A5266` / `#1F3E4F` — deep teal / petrol blue (primary base, 25-35%)
- `#D9A832` / `#C49220` — mustard yellow (secondary warm, 10-20%)
- `#6B3E8C` / `#4E2A6B` — dusty purple (night skies, 10-20%)
- `#F2E9D8` / `#E8DCC0` — warm cream paper (highlights, 5-10%)
- **Тенденция:** warm retro 4-5 color palette, screen-print-inspired. НИКОГДА pure black — всегда warm dark (burgundy/navy/olive).

## Typography
- **Minimal in the art itself** — типографика обычно внешняя (credits, movie title at top/bottom)
- When used: **70s sci-fi geometric sans** (Eurostile, ITC Avant Garde, Futura)
- Occasional heavy slab serif (для retro printed feel)
- Rarely hand-lettered
- **Movie poster convention** — title top OR bottom, cast list in small caps, credit block at bottom (classic movie poster layout)

## Composition & Grid
- **Central hero subject** — vehicle / spacecraft / figure
- Ornamental **frame/border motifs** (reminiscent of concert posters, tarot cards)
- **Horizon-anchored** landscapes часто (two-thirds sky, one-third ground)
- Dense midground details — many visual points to discover
- Symmetric OR asymmetric — both work

## Signature Moves
- **Screen-print grain** — visible halftone / noise texture through палитру
- **Color separation artifacts** (fake or real CMYK misregistration)
- **Ornamental frames** — decorative borders around focal area
- **Celestial bodies** — suns, moons, planets (often multiple)
- **Retro sci-fi vehicles** — rounded 70s forms, not angular 80s cyberpunk
- **Alien landscapes** — hills, deserts, unusual geology
- **Silhouetted figures** for scale против huge landscape
- **Dithered gradients** — not smooth, but stepped/grainy
- **Limited-palette discipline** — looks like 3-5 color print

## Techniques / Tools
- Digital (Photoshop / Procreate) но **mimicking traditional print**
- Grain/noise textures laid over clean vector
- Hand-drawn feel в digital execution
- Often exports to matte paper actual prints for Mondo

## Mood / Emotional Register
Nostalgic, contemplative, cinematic. Wonder + melancholy. "Future we imagined in 1975." Less cynical than cyberpunk, warmer than Thorp.

## When to apply
- ✅ Alternative movie posters, genre film marketing
- ✅ Sci-fi / fantasy book covers
- ✅ Retro-styled concert / festival posters
- ✅ Когда нужна warm nostalgia + epic scale
- ❌ НЕ для corporate / clean modern
- ❌ НЕ для cold cyber (это Thorp)
- ❌ НЕ для minimal (Kilian = ornamental)

## How to apply (tactical для Claude)
1. **Палитра: 4-5 warm retro colors.** Orange + teal + mustard + purple + cream. НЕ pure black, используй warm-dark.
2. **Композиция: hero object** в центре, ornamental frame вокруг, landscape ниже.
3. **Add grain/noise** overlay на всё (imitate screen-print).
4. **Multiple celestial bodies** — 2 suns, moon rings, planets в небе.
5. **Horizon line** two-thirds down — огромное небо.
6. **Silhouetted tiny figure** для scale контраст с hero object.
7. **Decorative border motifs** — thin geometric frames, concert-poster-adjacent.
8. **Title at top OR bottom** — classic movie poster layout.
9. **НЕ использовать:** flat modern vector без texture, clean monochrome minimalism, neon cyberpunk palette.
