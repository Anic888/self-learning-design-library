Six posters, each anchored to a distinct **cultural theme** (cinema / art / music / games / hi-tech / electronic music) and built as **designer style × 2026 visual trend**. Deliberately varied palettes so the series doesn't collapse into one register.

## Why different palettes

Previous showcase ([showcase-2026-04](../showcase-2026-04/)) proved designer profiles produce **legibly distinct outputs** by changing only the style reference. This themed series proves something further: that the same library supports **genuinely different palette registers** across themes without losing author voice per piece.

Posters 1–4 share a warm-paper + dark-anchor + oxblood family — the "tactile print" register. Poster 5 jumps to deep-space + neon-data (Anadol's native territory). Poster 6 lands in jet-black monochrome + Factory-yellow pop (Saville's Factory DNA). Three palette registers in six pieces — same library, three very different outputs.

## What each piece demonstrates concept-wise

### 01 — ARRAKIS: THE SILENT SUN (cinema)
Fictional arthouse sci-fi film poster. Mondo-tradition alternative movie poster with classical-serif Dark Academia typography (drop cap, small caps, italic tagline). Three celestial bodies + tiny silhouette figure + dither grain = Kilian Eng Mode 1 Warm Mondo canon.

### 02 — AFTER THE MACHINE (art)
Tate Modern exhibition on post-AI art. Appropriated classical painting (Caravaggio-lit hand with pixel-dissolving fingertips) carries the whole image — Saville's "appropriated-image dominance". Frosted glass spatial-UI card carries type. Iridescent accent mark = the 2026 Vision-Pro finish.

### 03 — BLUE NOTHING (jazz album)
Blue Note jazz quartet album. Vinyl record cropped off right edge (maximalism bleed). Label uses classic Blue Note vocabulary (red label, gold ring, spindle hole, arc-text). Hand-scribble below ensemble list = Sagmeister "see the hand".

### 04 — ASHFALL: Kingdom of Ruin (game)
FromSoftware-adjacent soulslike. Lone ink-brushed knight kneeling with sword planted. Torii gate ruin fading into pale dreamcore mist (wrong-scale: gate huge, knight tiny). Oxblood blood-splatter at sword tip + red 灰 seal = Shinkawa monochrome discipline bent just enough to include the dreamcore wash.

### 05 — COMPOSE 2026 (AI conference)
Three-day AI developer conference. Aurora mesh gradient (4 colored blooms heavily blurred) + point-cloud overlay (~100 dots with glow on anchors) = Anadol Mode 1 Neon Data rendered at poster scale. Sharp SF-Pro-esque white typography over soft color field.

### 06 — KOMPAKT 25 (electronic label)
25-year retrospective for fictional Kompakt Records. Pure typography — no imagery. Giant "25" at 340pt. Factory-yellow horizontal bar through the numerals. Label-style catalog number stamp. Peter Saville's Factory DNA rendered with Variable Bold Typography 2026 finish.

## How to use these as starting points

Each SVG is heavily commented. Open in any text editor to see:
- The grid plan (pre-measured vertical anchors) — at the top of every file
- Which hex codes belong to which style profile (palette lock)
- How the trend layer is applied (gradients, filters, overlays)
- Composition rules enforced (safe margins, text-width calculations)

To adapt for your own subject: swap the subject text, keep the structural decisions and palette. Don't mix palettes across pieces — that's how the 2026-era look collapses.
